Question 2

set.seed(1)
x1 = runif(10, min = -1, max = 1)
mean(x1)
## [1] 0.1030277
x2 = runif(100, min = -1, max = 1)
mean(x2)
## [1] 0.03034921
x3 = runif(1000, min = -1, max = 1)
mean(x3)
## [1] -0.007201965

We can conclude that the sample mean gets closer to the true mean as the sample size increases.
